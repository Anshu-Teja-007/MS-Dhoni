MS Dhoni

Former Indian international cricketer who captained the Indian national team.

Dhoni is a right-handed batsman and wicket-keeper.He is famous for his helicopter shot and his wicket-keeping skill is widely praised by cricket experts and has a world record for the highest number of stumpings

Personal life

  1. Dhoni married Sakshi Singh Rawat, his schoolmate in DAV Jawahar Vidya Mandir, Shyamali and a native of Dehradun, Uttarakhand, on 4 July 2010.
  2. Dhoni became a father on 6 February 2015 to a baby girl named Ziva. At the time of her birth, Dhoni was in Australia as the captain of the Indian team with 2015 Cricket World Cup only a week away. He decided not to travel back to India and was famously quoted saying that "I am on national duty, other things can wait."
